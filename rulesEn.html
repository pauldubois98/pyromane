The Pyromaniac Game

In this game, you play a pyromaniac who wants to burn down a forest. As you are lazy, you want to light as few fires as possible, while burning as many trees as possible.

Fire propagation rules:

A tree (green square) catches fire only if it is adjacent (1 adjacent side) to a fire (red square).

Where to put the initial fires:

To begin the party, the player will place strategically one or several fires (orange squares).

Standard Game:

When the fire is started, each burned tree earns 1 point, while each fire placed at the beginning costs points (10 by default).

One fire:

The pyromaniac player has 5 seconds to place a single fire, intended to burn as many trees as possible.

Multi-players:

Players play each turn on the same forest, the one with the best score wins ... Luck is no longer an excuse!

Settings:

It is possible to adjust the dimensions of the forest.
By default, it is a 20 square-sided forest.
